Swift


Swift is an open source programming language being developed primarily by Apple. It packs a lot of really cool features and is starting to gain traction from developers working across a variety of different platforms. Swift has a bright future and many companies such as Google, Facebook, and Uber are considering making Swift an integral part of their operations. For instance, Google is considering Swift as a viable option for the primary language for Android.
